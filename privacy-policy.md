# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 11, 2026**

Navora ("the app") is developed by Santiago Garza ("I", "me"). This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

## Data Collection

Navora does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All trip data you create is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

## Sign In with Apple

Navora offers optional sign-in using Sign In with Apple. When you sign in:

- Apple provides your name and email address directly to the app — they are stored locally on your device only.
- I do not operate a backend server and never receive or store your credentials.
- You can sign out or delete your account at any time via **Profile → Manage Account**.

## Subscriptions

Navora offers auto-renewing subscriptions (monthly and yearly) processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. I do not collect or store any payment information. Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID.

## Notifications

If you enable trip reminders, Navora schedules local notifications on your device. No notification data is transmitted externally.

## Third-Party SDKs

Navora does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Children's Privacy

Navora is not directed at children under 13. I do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

I may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
